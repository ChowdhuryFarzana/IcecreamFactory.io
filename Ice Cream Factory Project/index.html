About Us

In the mystical land of Frosthaven, where snowflakes sparkled like stardust, an ice cream factory of wonder emerged—the Celestial Scoopery. Run by the mystical sorcerer, Master Gelato, it defied the laws of nature. Beneath shimmering auroras, he conjured frozen fantasies, infusing each scoop with celestial magic. Moonlight vanilla, starfire strawberry, and nebulae chocolate enchanted taste buds. The factory's enchanted machines hummed harmoniously, churning dreams into reality. Once a year, the Great Frost Festival saw mystical beings gather to savor the divine creations. Legends spoke of the Celestial Scoopery's power to bestow fleeting flights to the heavens—a place where ice cream tasted like ethereal dreams.

Our History

In the forgotten annals of Frosthaven's history, the Celestial Scoopery weaved a tale of mystical legacy. Eons ago, a sorceress of ice and stars, Lady Celestia, harnessed the cosmos' essence to forge the factory's foundations. Her magic resonated through enchanted crystals, empowering gelato artisans with celestial skills. As generations passed, each ruler of the Gelato lineage carried the weight of stars in their bloodline, preserving the sacred recipes. Through wars and cosmic upheavals, the Celestial Scoopery endured, a beacon of hope in frozen times. Those blessed by its ethereal creations were said to glimpse the heavens, connected to the celestial tapestry through the very taste of enchantment.
